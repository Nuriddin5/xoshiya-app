## Manual Verification Steps
1. **Start Recording:**
   - Select a desktop source.
   - Click "Start".
   - Verify status becomes `recording` and "Chunk 1" appears.
2. **Pause Capture:**
   - Click "Pause" while Chunk 1 is recording.
   - Verify status becomes `paused`.
   - Verify status message says "Paused (Transcription catching up...)" if Chunk 1 is still transcribing.
   - Verify no new chunk (Chunk 2) is started.
3. **Resume Capture:**
   - Click "Resume".
   - Verify status returns to `recording`.
   - Verify "Chunk 2" starts.
4. **Finalize Session:**
   - Click "Stop".
   - Verify status becomes `stopped`.
   - Inspect "Raw transcript" to confirm both chunks are present in order.
5. **Persistence Check:**
   - Start a recording, then click "Pause".
   - Refresh the Electron window (e.g., via `Ctrl+R` in dev mode).
   - Verify the dashboard restores the paused session state, including previous chunks and events.
   - Click "Resume" and verify capture continues as Chunk 3 (if 2 chunks were recorded before).
