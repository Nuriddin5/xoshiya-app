## Manual UI Check

1. Run `npm run dev`.
2. Open the Electron window and go to Dashboard.
3. Choose a desktop source that provides audio.
4. Click `Start`.
5. Wait for at least one chunk interval to complete, or stop after a short recording to force the last chunk flush.
6. Confirm the lifecycle log shows chunk events.
7. Confirm the `Chunk records` section appears below the lifecycle log.
8. Confirm each non-empty chunk shows a state such as `saving` or `saved`.
9. Confirm each saved chunk shows a local filesystem path under the system temp directory, inside `study-capture/audio-chunks`.
10. Confirm no network upload step exists and no audio is sent outside the app.
11. If a chunk has no audio data, confirm it appears as `failed` instead of creating a saved file path.
